Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information is and isn't collected when you use tools.ifscpincode.in.

1. Tools run in your browser

The vast majority of our tools — calculators, formatters, encoders, generators and converters — process everything locally in your web browser. The data you type or paste (numbers, text, code, files) is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

2. Information we collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the tools. We collect only standard, anonymous analytics to understand how the site is used and to improve it.

3. Analytics

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled. This helps us see aggregate, non-identifying statistics such as which tools are popular and which devices visitors use. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies and local storage entries — for example, to remember your light/dark theme preference and for anonymous analytics. You can clear these at any time through your browser settings.

5. Advertising

If advertising is shown on this site, third-party vendors such as Google may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ads Settings.

6. Third-party links

Our pages may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

7. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will always reflect the latest revision.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through our Contact page.
