What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator computes your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth to today (or any target date you choose). It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths, giving you a precise result that simple subtraction cannot provide.

Why Use an Online Age Calculator?

Knowing your exact age to the day is useful in many situations — filling government forms, checking age eligibility for exams like TNPSC, SSC, UPSC, or jobs, applying for loans, insurance, or pension schemes, and confirming age for medical records. This free tool gives instant, accurate results without any sign-up or installation required.

How to Use This Age Calculator

Enter your date of birth and select the date you want to calculate your age as of (it defaults to today). The calculator will instantly show your age in years, months, and days. You can also use it in reverse — to calculate someone else's age or to find the age difference between two dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the calculator handle leap years?

The calculator accounts for leap years (years divisible by 4, except century years unless divisible by 400) automatically. So if you were born on 29 February, it correctly calculates your age on non-leap years as well.

Can I calculate age in months or days only?

Yes. The result shows the complete breakdown — total years, remaining months, and remaining days. This is especially useful for parents tracking a baby's growth milestones or for doctors calculating a patient's age in months.

What is the age limit for UPSC and SSC exams?

For UPSC Civil Services, the minimum age is 21 years and maximum is 32 years (with relaxations for OBC/SC/ST). For SSC CGL, the age range is 18–32 years depending on the post. Use this calculator to verify your exact eligibility age as of the exam's cut-off date.
