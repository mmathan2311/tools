About Us

Free, fast, privacy-first tools for everyone.

tools.ifscpincode.in is a free collection of online calculators and developer utilities built to be fast, accurate and respectful of your privacy. It is a companion project to ifscpincode.in, which helps people look up IFSC codes and PIN codes across India.

Our mission

We believe everyday tools should be instantly usable — no signups, no paywalls, no clutter and no harvesting of your data. Whether you are calculating a loan EMI, formatting JSON, generating a strong password or creating a QR code, the tool should just work.

Privacy by design

Almost every tool here runs entirely inside your browser. The numbers, text and files you enter are processed on your own device and are never uploaded to our servers. Read our Privacy Policy for the full details.

What you'll find

  • Finance calculators — EMI, GST, SIP, compound interest, discounts and percentages.
  • Health tools — BMI and age calculators.
  • Text tools — word and character counters, case converter and Lorem Ipsum.
  • Developer utilities — JSON/XML/HTML formatters, Base64, URL encoder, UUID, hashes and colors.
  • Everyday utilities — stopwatch & timer, password generator and QR codes.
