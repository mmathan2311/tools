EMI Calculator

Compute monthly EMI for any loan amount, interest rate and tenure. Includes amortization table and a cumulative principal/interest chart.

How to use

Enter your loan amount, annual interest rate and tenure in years. Click Calculate EMI to view the monthly payment amount, total interest payable and a full amortization breakdown. Use the CSV button to export the schedule for record keeping.
