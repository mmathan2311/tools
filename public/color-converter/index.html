HEX, RGB and HSL explained

HEX codes (e.g. #4F46E5) are the most common way to define colors in CSS. RGB describes a color by its red, green and blue channels. HSL uses hue, saturation and lightness, which makes it easy to create lighter or darker variations of a color. This tool keeps all three in sync as you adjust the picker.
