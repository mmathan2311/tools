GST Calculator

Calculate GST amount for a given base price or compute base price from total (inclusive of GST). Supports common GST slabs.

Usage

Use this calculator for quick GST calculations when preparing invoices or quotes. Choose intra-state (CGST+SGST) or inter-state (IGST) as needed.

What is a GST Calculator?

A GST (Goods and Services Tax) calculator helps businesses, traders, and individuals quickly compute the tax amount on any product or service in India. GST was introduced on 1 July 2017 and replaced multiple indirect taxes. This free online GST calculator supports all GST slabs — 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28% — and handles both intra-state (CGST + SGST) and inter-state (IGST) transactions.

How GST is Calculated

For an exclusive GST calculation: GST Amount = Base Price × GST Rate / 100. For example, if the base price is ₹1,000 and GST rate is 18%, the GST amount is ₹180 and total price is ₹1,180. For a reverse (inclusive) calculation: Base Price = Total Price / (1 + GST Rate / 100).

CGST vs SGST vs IGST

For intra-state sales (within the same state), GST is split equally into CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST). For example, at 18% GST on an intra-state sale, CGST = 9% and SGST = 9%. For inter-state sales (across different states), the full GST is collected as IGST (Integrated GST). Our calculator handles both scenarios automatically.

Who Needs a GST Calculator?

Shop owners, freelancers, manufacturers, e-commerce sellers, and service providers all need to calculate GST on their invoices. This tool saves time when preparing quotations, invoices, or filing monthly GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B). It is especially useful for small business owners and traders who handle high volumes of transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which GST slab applies to my product or service?

GST rates vary by category: 5% for essential items like packaged food, 12% for items like mobile phones and processed foods, 18% for most services and electronics, and 28% for luxury goods, cars, and tobacco. Check the official GST Council rate schedule for your specific product or service.

How do I calculate GST from a total inclusive price?

Use the "From Total Price" button on this calculator. Enter the total amount (inclusive of GST) and select the applicable rate. The tool will back-calculate and show you the original base price and the exact GST component.

What is the GST on services?

Most services in India are taxed at 18% GST. However, some services like healthcare, education, and export services are exempt or zero-rated. Restaurant services attract 5% GST (without ITC) or 18% for restaurants in hotels with room tariff above ₹7,500 per night.

Is GST applicable on exports?

No. Exports of goods and services are zero-rated under GST. Exporters can claim a refund of any GST paid on inputs used in manufacturing exported goods.
