How to calculate a discount

To find a discounted price, multiply the original price by the discount percentage to get the savings, then subtract it from the original price. For example, 25% off ₹2,000 saves ₹500, leaving a sale price of ₹1,500. This calculator also lets you add GST or sales tax to the discounted amount for the true final price.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate percentage off?

Savings = Original Price × Discount% ÷ 100. Final price = Original Price − Savings.

Can I add GST to the result?

Yes. Enter a tax percentage and it is applied to the discounted price to give the final payable amount.
